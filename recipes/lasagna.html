Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base.

The sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly!

This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

The meat sauce:

  • olive oil
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 Can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 Medium onion(finely chopped)
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon italian herbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Assembly

  • 500g of lasagna sheets (depending on size of dish you may need more or less
  • 3 balls of fresh mozzarella cheese.
  • 100g of parmesan cheese

Method

The meat sauce:

  1. Begin by browning the beef in medium-hig temperature, should take 3-t minutes. Once browned remove from pan along with an excess fat.
  2. Next begin frying the onions on a medium heat for 5 minutes. Then add salt, pepper garlic, italian seasoning and tomato paste. Fry this for a further 3 minutes.
  3. Then add the can of chopped tomatoes and simmer for another 5 minutes before returning the mince to the pan.
  4. Keep this on a simmer for 30-45 minutes or until the mince is tender. potentially add a little water if it becomes to thick
  5. Once finished set aside to cool for 10 minutes before assembly.

For Assembly

  1. Add quater of your meat sauce to the bottom of an oven proof dish.
  2. Next break a some mozzarella and spread evenly over the sauce.
  3. Then cover the cheese and sauce with dried pasta sheets.
  4. Repeat these steps 2 more times to have 3 even layers.
  5. On the very top of the lasagna spread the last bit of your meat sauce along with the grated parmesan.
  6. Put in a preheated oven at 180 degrees and let cook from 25-35 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
  7. Once ready take out of the oven allowed to rest for 20 minutes before slicing and serving!
